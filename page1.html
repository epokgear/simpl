FAQ



> What is Simpl Supply?


Simpl Supply is a wholesale supplier of event and party goods. Items meant for parties such as: glow in the dark, pool party, birthday party, barmitzvah's, corporate events and anything in between.


In addition we offer a concierge to help you come up with event product choices.



> How Do I get started?


Just text us (770.231.4896) what kind of event you are having and what theme you want to keep with and we'll start making suggestions.

Everyone you text is REAL. No artifical intelligence here!  



> Are there any fees?


No fees to use our concierge at all!



> Where are the products sourced from?


They are sourced globally so we can not only get you the best prices, but also the most unique items and provide worldwide free shipping.



> Do you have a minimum order amount?


Yes. Since we mainly only work with businesses and large events, we do have a minimum order of $100.



> Can you fit within my budget?


Yes if you give us a heads up during planning, we can help you stay inside your budget for all your supplies



> What is your return policy?


We have a 30 day return policy on all unopened, unused and unbroken items. We do not cover return shipping.



> What is your shipping policy?


We offer free worldwide shipping on all orders!



> How far in advance should I begin planning with you?


We always suggest around 3 months to plan and get all the supplies there ahead of time. 2 Months we could do at a push. And one month if it's a last second thing.  The early bird catches the worm :)


> How soon do you respond with suggestions?


We try to get to each incoming response in record time. Unforuntaley since we don't have AI running the scene it can take up to 2 hours to get a response with suggestions.


> What are your hours of operation?


Our concierge works Mon- Thurs 8am to 8pm and Saturdays from 9am till 4pm.
